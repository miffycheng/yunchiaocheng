London Bike Rides Analysis

This dashboard provides an insightful analysis of bike-sharing trends in London, leveraging the London Bike Sharing Dataset from Kaggle. The dataset was cleaned and processed using Python to ensure accuracy before visualization. The dashboard includes a heatmap that highlights bike usage patterns across different times of the day, week, and month. A moving average chart illustrates long-term trends and seasonality in ridership. Additionally, the total rides metric offers an overview of overall bike usage, while bar charts analyze rides based on factors such as weather conditions and wind speed. This visualization provides valuable insights for transportation planners, policymakers, and businesses looking to optimize bike-sharing services.
